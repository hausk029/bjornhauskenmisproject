My UMD Journey

Exploring life at the University of Minnesota Duluth

Why I Chose UMD

I chose UMD because my family has always come up to the North Shore for fall break and I grew to love the area. It is also the perfect size and distance from home.

Life in Duluth

What I love about Duluth and it's surroundings is all the beauty it contains. The lakes. The falls. The trees. The rocks. The nature is a big draw. Another great part about the city is that it is a good size, not too big and not too small.

My UMD Experience

Some initial cool experiences I have had on campus are going to both my first UMD football game and first UMD hockey game. Also I have enjoyed playing Ultimate Frisbee on Fridays and taking walks around Canal Park on the weekends.
